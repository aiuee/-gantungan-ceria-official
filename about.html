About Us

Get to know Rantai Ceria!

Who We Are

Rantai Ceria is a student-led project from the class "Kreativitas, Inovasi, dan Kewirausahaan." Our goal is to bring smiles and fun to people through our cute and quirky keychains and accessories.

Our Mission

  • To offer fun and affordable keychains for all ages
  • To learn and practice real business and marketing skills
  • To express creativity and teamwork in every product

Our Team

We are a group of international students working together to create, design, and sell products. Every item you see is made with teamwork and lots of love!
